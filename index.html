WHY HACKATHON?

A hackathon is a collaborative event where innovators solve real-world problems, fostering creativity, skill development, and networking. It offers career growth, mentorship, and opportunities to create impactful solutions. It empower the participants through innovation, inclusivity, and real-world challenges, driving meaningful change.
